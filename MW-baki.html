Things You Should Know About Baki


"Baki" is a thrilling and action-packed anime that revolves around the protagonist, Baki Hanma, who trains intensely to surpass his father, Yujiro Hanma, the strongest fighter in the world


Here are some reasons why you might want to watch "Baki":


  • Engaging and Unique Fight Scenes: The series features intense and captivating fight scenes, showcasing Baki's journey as he faces various opponents in deadly, no-rules hand-to-hand combat

  • Global Setting: The story takes place around the world, with Baki traveling to different locations to hone his martial arts skills and challenge the best fighters

  • Strong and Unique Characters: "Baki" introduces a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique abilities and strengths, making the series more engaging and entertaining

  • Underground Fighting Arena: The series features an underground fighting arena where Baki fights some of the most powerful opponents, showcasing his growth and development as a martial artist

  • Deep and Emotional Connections: The anime delves into the emotional depths of its characters and their relationships, adding a layer of complexity and depth to the story
