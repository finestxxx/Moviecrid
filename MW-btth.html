Along the way, he also encounters love and romance, as he reunites with his childhood sweetheart Xiao Xun'er, who is the descendant of the Gu tribe, a mysterious race that can control beasts. He also befriends Xiao Yixian, the daughter of the Ice King, who has a rare and deadly poison in her body. Together, they face many dangers and challenges, from ancient beasts and powerful sects, to hidden secrets and dark conspiracies, to the ultimate enemy: the legendary Dracula, the lord of all vampires.
