Name: Kengan Ashura

Year: 2019-2023

Status: Completed

Rating(IMDb): 8.0/10


Plot


"Kengan Ashura" is a Japanese manga and anime series that revolves around the underground world of corporate gladiator fights. The story follows Ohma Tokita, a powerful fighter who enters this hidden world where corporate disputes are settled in brutal bouts. The series is known for its intense and violent fighting scenes, unique fighting styles, and fast-paced storytelling. The anime adaptation is primarily in 3D with some 2D animation mixed in, and it offers a visually immersive experience. The plot is centered around the Kengan matches, where combatants fight to the death, and the main character, Ohma, becomes a central figure in this high-stakes and violent arena. The series is recommended for those who enjoy action-packed martial arts anime with a focus on intense combat and unique fighting abilities. If you're a fan of shows like "Baki" and enjoy stylized fights and nonstop visceral action, "Kengan Ashura" may be a compelling watch for you.
