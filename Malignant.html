Malignant (2021)

Genre: Horror, Action, Supernatural


Synopsis:"Malignant" is a gripping horror-thriller that takes audiences on a twisted journey of suspense, mystery, and supernatural terror. Madison Mitchell, a young woman living a troubled life, starts experiencing horrific visions of brutal murders. To her horror, she soon discovers that these terrifying events are not mere hallucinations but are actually happening in reality. As the body count rises, Madison finds herself directly connected to the heinous acts, tormented by an unseen force. Desperate for answers, Madison seeks help from her sister Sydney, a detective, and her supportive husband Derek. Together, they delve into their family's dark past and uncover a chilling secret that connects Madison to a malevolent presence from her childhood. As Madison's connection to the sinister force deepens, she becomes a target for its relentless pursuit. With time running out and her loved ones' lives at stake, Madison must confront her own traumatic memories and face the evil that has plagued her since her Youth
