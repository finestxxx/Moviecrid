#15 Anime With Overpowered MC

Anime is known for its incredible storytelling and relatable characters. However, there is nothing quite like an anime with overpowered main character, who takes the spotlight and dominates the story. Anime with OP MC, is a sub genre that has gained a significant following in recent years. But how do we know an anime character is overpowered? It is very simple; they have extraordinary strength, abilities, and powers that far surpass those of other characters in the series. Most often, this overpowered character is transported to a fantasy world and, through various means, acquires god-like powers. This genre is filled with action-packed battles, intense drama, and a sense of power fantasy that fans can’t get enough of. Another trope commonly found in anime with op main character is the idea of a harem. The protagonist often finds himself surrounded by beautiful and powerful women who are drawn to his strength and potential. This trope adds an element of romance and comedy to the series and creates a dynamic that keeps audiences hooked.
So without further adieu, let us jump into the list.

1.How Not To Summon A Demon Lord

Are you craving to see an anime that has an insanely-powerful protagonist along with a hint of echhi and comedy? If yes, look no further than “How Not To Summon A Demon Lord”! “How Not To Summon A Demon Lord” is an anime that perfectly personifies the trope of an anime with overpowered main character. It follows the story of Takuma Sakamoto, a socially awkward gamer who gets transported to a parallel world where he becomes his in-game character, a demon lord also known as Diablo. The unexpected here is that Sakamoto was actually summoned by two ladies from this otherworld to make him their slave. However their magic backfires instead and he turns out becoming their master.

The anime presents a thrilling demon hunting storyline and also includes a balance of fan service and steamy scenes, making it an entertaining and engaging watch. The story is filled with unexpected twists and turns, however the isekai’d demon lord is at hand to save the day. No matter how much equipment the enemies have or what their level is, Diablo can easily defeat them. Not only this, he even admits that he holds back when facing opponents. This is a true testament to his abilities. If you’re looking for a show that combines the excitement of an anime with OP MC with the guilty pleasure of ecchi elements, then “How Not to Summon a Demon Lord” is definitely worth checking out.

2.Hellsing Ultimate

Title:

Title:

Title:

If you’re a fan of action, horror, and strong main characters, Hellsing Ultimate is an anime with OP MC you won’t want to miss. This series is packed with thrilling action and a hint of horror that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

The show follows the story of the Hellsing Organization, a secret agency that fights against supernatural threats to England, and its leader, Alucard, a vampire who is also the show’s main protagonist. The series is filled with intense battles between Alucard and his foes, which include other vampires, werewolves, and ghouls.

Alucard has a vast array of extraordinary abilities, including immense strength, speed, and agility, as well as the power to quickly heal from injuries. Additionally, he has the ability to control and manipulate other vampires. With all these abilities, he can easily defeat any foe that stands in his way. Beyond the action and horror, Hellsing Ultimate also delves into deeper themes such as the nature of good and evil and the blurred lines between them.

The anime is also filled with political undertones, which makes it a perfect watch for viewers who are looking for something more than just mindless entertainment. The series raises thought-provoking questions about the morality of using monstrous beings like Alucard to fight against other monsters. With a well-crafted story, likable characters, and intense action, Hellsing Ultimate is a must-watch for any anime fan.

Irregular at Magic High is an anime that is sure to capture your attention and leave you wanting more. The plot follows a group of students at a magic high school, where the students are trained to become powerful magic users.

5.Rurouni Kenshin

Title:

Title:

Title:

Title:

So you’re a fan of samurai anime and want to watch an anime with OP MC that’s a samurai? Then the next entry on our list is sure to make you add this to your watch list.

Rurouni Kenshin, also known as Samurai X, is an anime that stands out from the rest due its unique setting and premise.

The main character, Kenshin Himura, is a powerful swordsman with a tragic past. He is known as the “Hitokiri Battōsai,” a ruthless killer who fought for the revolution. But after the war, Kenshin renounces violence and becomes a wanderer, vowing to never kill again. Rurouni Kenshin perfectly balances Kenshin’s past and present. Unlike other OP Mc anime, we get to witness the main character’s guilt and struggles as he tries to make amends for his actions. The sword fights are intense and well-animated, with Kenshin’s unique “Hiten Mitsurugi-ryū” style standing out. Kenshin is a foe that you might want to avoid. His quick sword movements and ability to anticipate his opponent’s action make him a deadly opponent.

The historical setting of the anime also adds depth and realism to the story, as it portrays the struggles of the Meiji era in Japan, such as the rise of western influence and the struggles of the common people. Get ready for an epic journey through 19th century Japan with Rurouni Kenshin.

6.Eminence In The Shadow

Title:

Title:

Title:

Title:

Eminence in the Shadow tells the story of a high school student named Minoru Kagenou who gets transported to a different world after being hit by a truck.

Despite his unassuming appearance, Minoru Kagenou (who is now known as Cid) possesses a powerful ability known as “Eminence” that allows him to control and manipulate shadows. The Eminence in the Shadow is a bit different compared to other anime of this genre. The main character is an overpowered individual who boasts not only incredible physical strength but also exceptional intelligence and strategic thinking. However, what sets him apart from others is their strong desire to avoid being in the spotlight and maintain a low profile.

There is also a whole new level of misunderstanding going on in Cid’s world as the seemingly fake Diablo cult he created turns out to be true. His army of minions (comprised entirely of femme fatale who worship him) trust his word and fight the Diablo cult, however to Cid, all of these are just made up stories and it makes for an interesting read. As Cid navigates through the challenges, he discovers that his abilities are not just a blessing but also a curse, as he is targeted by a secret organization that wants to harness his powers for their own gain.

Despite being an overpowered main character, Minoru Kagenou is a relatable protagonist who struggles with his own identity and the weight of his abilities. The animation is visually stunning, with the use of shadows and lighting adding to the atmosphere of the show.

8.Assassination Classroom

Title:

Title:

Title:

Title:

Assassination Classroom follows a group of students who are tasked with assassinating their powerful and mysterious teacher, who just so happens to be an alien with incredible abilities. But here’s the twist: the alien teacher, Koro-sensei, isn’t the villain of the story. Instead, he’s a lovable and charismatic character who is dedicated to helping his students become strong and capable assassins. This might sound like a strange premise, but trust us, it’s a lot of fun.

Assassination Classroom subverts expectations and challenges traditional notions of what it means to be a hero or a villain. Koro-sensei is an overpowered character, but instead of using his powers for evil, he uses them to teach and mentor his students. In fact, he challenges them all to become powerful enough to assassinate him, which is a breath of fresh air when it comes to anime with op main characters. The show is packed with action, humour, and heart. The students are all unique and likable, and their growth throughout the series is a real treat to watch. But the real star of the show is the alien teacher, who is equal parts terrifying and endearing. His backstory and motivations are revealed slowly over time, and they are truly fascinating.

You would think that a story about a group of students trying to kill their teacher would be dark and gritty, but you’ll find that this anime has a surprise or two in store for you. And the fact that the teacher is an overpowered character makes it all the more exciting.

9.That Time I got Reincarnated as a Slime

Title:

Title:

Title:

Title:

Slime is one of the popular titles in this genre featuring overpowered main characters, and does not need any special introduction. The story follows the journey of Satoru Mikami, a 37-year-old man who is reincarnated as a slime in a fantasy world. The anime is set in a fantasy world where the main character, Rimuru Tempest, the reincarnation of Satoru Mikami, is a slime. However, don’t let the slime’s cute appearance fool you as he turns out to be one of the most overpowered beings over the course of the story.

He has the ability to absorb the abilities of any creature he defeats, making him a formidable force to be reckoned with. He also discovers that he can communicate with monsters, which leads to him forming an alliance with them and building a city for them to live in. Rimuru has unparalleled strength and possesses immunity to virtually all forms of harm. His ability to consume and transform into any substance allows him to adapt to any situation, and his ability to revive fallen allies ensures that no one is truly defeated. Furthermore, Rimuru’s power extends to the creation of objects from memory, making him one of the most overpowered MCs in existence.

Rimuru’s journey is one of self-discovery as he figures out how to use his powers for the betterment of others and the world around him. The anime is filled with action and adventure, as Rimuru and his friends go on quests, fight monsters, and make allies with other races.

11.One Punch Man

Title:

Title:

Title:

Title:

Let’s be honest; we all know we expected One Punch Man to be here on the list, and it’s not surprising that One Punch Man is second on our anime with OP MC list. The story follows the journey of Saitama, an overpowered hero who can defeat any opponent with a single punch. But despite his incredible strength, Saitama is bored with his lack of challenge and sets out to find a worthy opponent.

The show is filled with over-the-top battles and hilarious moments that will have you laughing and cheering at the same time. Saitama’s deadpan delivery and complete lack of interest in his own power make for a comedic contrast to his enemies’ overconfidence. One Punch Man is a parody of the typical shonen anime, with its main character being so overpowered that he can defeat any enemy in one punch, making the show a hilarious satire of the genre.

Additionally, the series also has a great cast of characters, including Saitama’s cyborg apprentice Genos, the ninja Speed-o’-Sound Sonic, and the powerful S-class heroes of the Hero Association. The animation is top-notch, and the humour is spot-on. With its unique take on the typical action anime, One Punch Man is a must-watch for fans of the genre and newcomers alike. Saitama, the main character of the series is always compared with Goku of Dragon Ball and fans debate about who is stronger than who all the time. However, many believe that there are several characters in Dragon Ball who can defeat Saitama and they are not part of the God pantheon in the series.

12.The Disastrous Life of Saiki K

Title:

Title:

Title:

Title:

The title that takes the top spot in our list of anime with OP main character is none other than The Disastrous Life of Saiki K. This anime follows Kusuo Saiki, a high school student with psychic abilities that could make him one of the strongest characters out there. But instead of using his powers to save the world or defeat enemies, Kusuo just wants to live a normal life and avoid attention. All Saiki wants to do is get through high school without any drama. There are a couple of reasons why this anime tops the list for us. The first being Saiki’s ridiculous psychic abilities, which make him an almost omnipotent being. From mind reading and teleporting, to bending space-time and spawning/destroying entire universes (without his limiters), Saiki clearly is the most powerful protagonist in this list.

The second is the fact that the anime is once again something that avoids all the cliches and gives us a heart-warming and extremely funny narrative. Saiki K also has a relatable theme of acceptance and fitting in, which is a nice change of pace from the usual “save the world” trope.

Its take on the anime with overpowered main character trope is fresh and hilarious, and the relatable themes and well-written characters make it a must-watch for any anime fan.
