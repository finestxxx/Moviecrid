The Witch subversion (2018)

Genre: Horror, Action, Supernatural


Synopsis:The Witch Subversion is a South Korean action-thriller film that follows the story of a young girl named Ja-yoon who wakes up with amnesia and discovers that she possesses supernatural powers. She is taken in by a couple who help her hide from those who seek to exploit her abilities for their own gain. As she tries to uncover the truth about her past and her powers, Ja-yoon must confront a dangerous organization that wants to capture and control her. The film is known for its intense action scenes and its exploration of themes such as power and identity.
