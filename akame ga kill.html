Akame ga Kill

Genre: Action, fantasy, Martial arts


Synopsis Akame ga Kill is an anime series that follows Tatsumi as he fights the Empire and comes face-to-face with powerful weapons, enemy assassins, challenges to his own morals and values, and ultimately, what it truly means to be an assassin with a cause1. Tatsumi is a young countryside boy who sets out to the capital with the dream of becoming part of the country army. After being swindled by a beautiful woman, a generous stranger offers him a place to stay the night, but nothing but misfortune follows1. Night Raid is the covert assassination branch of the Revolutionary Army, an uprising assembled to overthrow Prime Minister Honest, whose avarice and greed for power has led him to take advantage of the child emperors inexperience.
