Castlevania: Nocturne

Genre:Action, Adventure, Animation, Fantasy, Horror, Thriler

Annette, a sorceress from the Caribbean, seeks out Richter Belmont, last descendant of the long-fabled family of vampire hunters, to lead the resistance.
