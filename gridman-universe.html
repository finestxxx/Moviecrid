Gridman Universe

Genre:Action, Mecha, Sci-Fi


Synopsis:Gridman Universe is an anime film that features characters from SSSS.Gridman and SSSS.Dynazenon. The film is about Yūta and his friends who fight kaiju with Gridman and DynaRex, while dealing with the phenomenon of overlapping universes. They also meet Yomogi and his friends who are transported to their world from another universe. Together, they try to stop the unknown force that is attacking Gridman’s consciousness and threatening to destroy all the universes he created
