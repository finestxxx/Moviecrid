kengan Ashura season 1 Part 2

Genre: Action, fantasy, Martial arts


Synopsis:Kengan Ashura is a Japanese manga and anime series that tells the story of a brutal underground fighting tournament known as the Kengan matches. The Kengan matches are held between powerful corporations who select fighters to represent them in one-on-one battles to determine the strongest corporation in Japan. The story follows the journey of Ohma Tokita, a young fighter who is scouted by a wealthy businessman named Nogi Hideki to represent his company in the Kengan matches. Ohma must navigate through the dangerous world of underground fighting, facing off against skilled opponents, including other powerful fighters and assassins who will stop at nothing to win. Throughout the series, Ohma builds relationships with other fighters, including his mentor, the legendary fighter known as the "Tiger's Vessel," and his friend, the charismatic fighter Kazuo Yamashita. Along the way, Ohma discovers the true purpose behind the Kengan matches and the secrets that lie within the corporate world. Kengan Ashura is known for its intense action scenes, complex characters, and intricate plot twists. It explores themes such as power, greed, and loyalty, making for a thrilling and thought-provoking story.
