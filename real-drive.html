Real Drive

Genre: Action, Sci-Fi


Synopsis:The Real Drive is a “neo-futuristic science-fiction suspense” story is set in A.D. 2061, when a new, more secure information network called the Material Network (or “Metal”) has taken over human society. However, aberrations begin to crop up as individuals chafe against the rigid system, and so-called “cyber divers” are dispatched to investigate these incidents. The the main cast consists; the cyber driver Mari, an “intuitive girl” named Minamo, the team’s head Eiichir?, the combat expert S?ta, and the android Horon.
