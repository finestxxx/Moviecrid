Shixiong A Shixiong

Genre:Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Historical, Martial Arts, Romance


Synopsis:Shixiong A Shixiong" is a Chinese anime series that follows the story of Li Changshou, a small cultivator who is reborn in ancient times before the Great God-Conferment War. To survive in the cruel Primordial Times, Li Changshou endeavors to avoid accumulating any karma. He meticulously plans his actions, conceals his true abilities, and hones his skills in escape techniques, poison refinement, and mystical arts. His intention is to remain hidden and secure in the mountains until his ascension. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when his master brings home a junior sister. This event sets in motion a series of events that challenge Li Changshou's plans and lead to unforeseen adventures. The anime is known for its historical, martial arts, and fantasy themes.
