Swallowed star season 2

Genre: Action, fantasy, Martial arts


SynopsisSwallowed Star is a story about a mysterious RR virus that appeared one day, plunging the world into disaster. Infectious animals transformed into terrifying creatures and invaded in large numbers, while humans built walls around the destruction and established the base city as the last human stronghold1. The story follows the typical plot and drama of any cultivation donghua out there, with its main protagonist Luo Feng, a young lad who has great potential and aspires to be a warrior to save humanity from the threat of monsters and improve the state of living of his family.
