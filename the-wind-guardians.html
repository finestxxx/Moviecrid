The Wind Guardians

Genre:Action, Adventure, Comedy, Demons, Drama, Fantasy, Historical, Super Power


Synopsis: It tells the story of a blind boy named Lang Ming who embarks on a journey to find his mother who disappeared after a demon attack. Along the way, he meets a mysterious girl who calls herself Little Goblin and learns about the ancient guardians who used the power of the wind to seal the four beasts from another world
